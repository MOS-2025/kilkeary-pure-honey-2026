From January 2026 our beekeeping classes will be open to all.

Beginner Beekeeping Classes

Our Beginner Beekeeping Classes are designed for those with little or no experience. This level covers the basics of beekeeping, including understanding the hive, bee behaviour, essential equipment, and seasonal hive management, providing a strong foundation to start your beekeeping journey with confidence.

Novice Beekeeping Classes

The Novice Beekeeping Classes are ideal for beekeepers who have some hands-on experience and wish to build their skills further. This course focuses on hive inspections, swarm prevention, disease awareness, and improving colony health through practical and guided learning.

Experienced Beekeeping Classes

Our Experienced Beekeeping Classes are tailored for those who are confident in hive management and want to refine their techniques. Topics include advanced hive management, queen rearing, honey production optimisation, and problem-solving based on real apiary situations.

Kilkeary Pure Honey

Kilkeary Pure Honey is a small, family-run honey producer rooted in the countryside of Ireland. Our bees forage naturally across unspoiled hedgerows, meadows, and farmland, producing honey that truly reflects the landscape it comes from. We are passionate about traditional beekeeping, sustainability, and letting nature do what it does best.
Every jar of Kilkeary Pure Honey is harvested with care, minimally processed, and never blended or overheated This ensures that the natural flavour, aroma, and beneficial properties of the honey are preserved just as the bees intended. From hive to jar, quality and authenticity are at the heart of everything we do.
