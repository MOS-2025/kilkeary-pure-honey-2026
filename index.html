Beginner Beekeeping Classes

Our Beginner Beekeeping Classes are designed for those with little or no experience. This level covers the basics of beekeeping, including understanding the hive, bee behaviour, essential equipment, and seasonal hive management, providing a strong foundation to start your beekeeping journey with confidence.

Novice Beekeeping Classes

The Novice Beekeeping Classes are ideal for beekeepers who have some hands-on experience and wish to build their skills further. This course focuses on hive inspections, swarm prevention, disease awareness, and improving colony health through practical and guided learning.

Experienced Beekeeping Classes

Our Experienced Beekeeping Classes are tailored for those who are confident in hive management and want to refine their techniques. Topics include advanced hive management, queen rearing, honey production optimisation, and problem-solving based on real apiary situations.

Kilkeary Pure Honey

Kilkeary Pure Honey is a small, family-run honey producer rooted in the countryside of Ireland. Our bees forage naturally across unspoiled hedgerows, meadows, and farmland, producing honey that truly reflects the landscape it comes from. We are passionate about traditional beekeeping, sustainability, and letting nature do what it does best.
Every jar of Kilkeary Pure Honey is harvested with care, minimally processed, and never blended or overheated This ensures that the natural flavour, aroma, and beneficial properties of the honey are preserved just as the bees intended. From hive to jar, quality and authenticity are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Guarantee

At Kilkeary Pure Honey, we believe real honey needs no additives,no shortcuts and no compromise.

  • 100% Pure and Natural – nothing added, nothing taken away
  • Locally Produced – supporting local biodiversity and pollination
  • Raw and Unpasteurised – retaining natural enzymes and nutrients
  • Sustainably Harvested – always leaving enough for the bees

Because our honey is raw and natural, it may crystallise over time. This is a natural process and a sign of quality. Gentle warming will return it to a liquid state if desired.

Our Honey

Our delicious honey varieties

Clover Honey

Clover Honey is a classic favourite, known for its mild, sweet taste and smooth finish. It is produced when bees forage on clover-rich pastures, resulting in a light and versatile honey suitable for all uses.

Ivy Honey

Ivy Honey is a distinctive, late-season honey with a rich, strong flavour. It is known for its creamy texture and fast crystallisation. With a deep, earthy taste, Ivy Honey is often favoured by those who enjoy a robust honey and is particularly popular for winter use.

Wildflower Honey

Wildflower Honey is a true expression of the local environment, created from a variety of native flowers blooming throughout the season. Each batch can vary slightly in flavour and colour, making it a unique and natural product every time.

Whitethorn Honey

Whitethorn Honey is a delicate and aromatic honey produced when bees forage on whitethorn blossoms in spring. It has a smooth, mild sweetness with subtle floral notes, making it an excellent everyday honey.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is your honey raw and unprocessed?

    Yes. All Kilkeary Pure Honey is raw and unpasteurised. We strain our honey only to remove natural debris such as wax, while keeping its natural enzymes, pollen, and flavour intact.

  • Why has my honey crystallised?

    Crystallisation is a completely natural process and a sign of pure, high-quality honey. Different honeys crystallise at different rates depending on their natural sugar content.

  • Do you add anything to your honey?

    No. We never add sugar, syrups, preservatives, or flavourings. Every jar contains 100% pure honey produced by our bees.

  • How should I store my honey?

    Honey is best stored at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. There is no need to refrigerate honey.
